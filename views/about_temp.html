The Birth of Habula

The idea for Habula was born in July, 2017 when founder, Kanakaraju Gullapudi, noticed that there were no straight forward options for engineers to know what's happening in their college and in the colleges around them. Kanakaraju Gullapudi came up with an idea to give engineering a completely new way of being social. After four months of design and development Habula made its debut and finally launched on a small basis.
